Blue Beetle is a superhero movie that follows Jaime Reyes, the third iteration of the Blue Beetle, as he gains superpowers when a blue alien Scarab attaches to him after returning home from college. The Scarab bestows Jaime with a suit of armor that's capable of extraordinary powers, forever changing his destiny as he becomes the superhero known as Blue Beetle. The series follows the adventures of Harley Quinn and her journey of self-discovery after breaking up with the Joker upon realizing that he never loved her. Harley intends to make a name for herself in Gotham City's criminal underworld, starting with the formation of her own crew, which consists of her best friend Poison Ivy, Clayface, Doctor Psycho, King Shark, and Sy Borgman.[1] During her attempts to prove herself as a competent villain, Harley faces her troubled past, gains self-confidence that helps her abolish the Joker's influence on her, and finds an autonomous identity.

Adventure Time

Adventure Time is an American animated television series that offers a unique and captivating storyline, blending elements of fantasy, adventure, and post-apocalyptic themes. The show follows the story of Finn Mertens, a young heroic boy, and his best friend and adoptive older brother Jake, a talking yellow dog with magical powers. Together, they embark on a series of surreal adventures in the post-apocalyptic Land of Ooo, which was ravaged by a cataclysmic event known as the "Mushroom War," a nuclear war that destroyed civilization a thousand years before the series' events .
