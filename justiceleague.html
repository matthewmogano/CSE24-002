The Justice League

Description: "Justice League" brings together DC Comics' iconic superheroes—Batman, Wonder Woman, Superman, The Flash, Aquaman, and Cyborg—as they join forces to defend Earth from a catastrophic threat. Fueled by the selfless sacrifice of Superman, Batman and Wonder Woman recruit a team of metahumans to combat the formidable villain, Steppenwolf, and his army of Parademons. As the League struggles to unite and harness their individual powers, they must confront their own inner conflicts while striving to protect the world from impending doom. Packed with action, camaraderie, and larger-than-life battles, "Justice League" is an epic cinematic adventure that showcases the enduring power of unity and heroism in the face of overwhelming adversity.
